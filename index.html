    How to Play

    Use up arrow and down arrow to move the soldier

    Use space bar to fire your laser (1 damage)

    Use r to fire your rocket (5 damage), limited to 20 shots

    Increase your score by shooting aliens

    2 health = 2 points

    1 health, faster = 1 point

    8 health, slower = 4 points

    Collect health packs to restore 3 health points

    Collect ammo crates to gain 3 rockets

    Music can be muted with mute button
